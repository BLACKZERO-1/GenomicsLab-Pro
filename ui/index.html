About Helix BioSystems

GenomicsLab Pro is developed by Helix BioSystems, a group dedicated to creating powerful, accessible, and secure computational tools for the modern biologist.

Our mission is to democratize high-performance computational biology, enabling researchers to uncover groundbreaking insights by providing intuitive software that runs entirely on their own hardware.
